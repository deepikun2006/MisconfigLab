Platform Capabilities

Built for Modern Merchants

Six specialized audits to ensure your storefront is secure, compliant, and ready to scale.

Checkout Integrity Shield

Audits your payment pages for missing security headers (like CSP) that allow hackers to inject Magecart skimming scripts and steal credit cards.

Customer Data Privacy

Scans your APIs to ensure endpoints aren't accidentally leaking Personally Identifiable Information (PII) or unencrypted order histories without authentication.

API & Cart Logic Security

Validates your backend cart logic. Checks for overly permissive CORS policies and missing rate limits that lead to price manipulation and inventory hoarding.

Admin Panel Hardening

Locates exposed backends (like `/wp-admin` or `/magento`) that lack MFA or IP restrictions, preventing massive credential stuffing takeovers.

PCI DSS Readiness

Maps every single vulnerability directly to PCI DSS requirements (like Req 4.2.1 or 6.4.1), proving to payment processors you take security seriously.

Actionable Developer Fixes

No generic "CVSS 7.5" jargon. We provide clear, plain-English remediation steps your engineering team can deploy immediately to protect revenue.
