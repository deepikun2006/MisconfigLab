Ensure every API endpoint verifying customer data demands strict authentication and authorization tokens. {% elif "Debug Mode" in f.issue %} When debug mode is left on in a live website, it acts like leaving your back door wide open with a sign showing exactly how your house is built. These are quick fixes with a big impact on your users' safety.
